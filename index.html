Felstead Cricket Club

Enjoy the class of cricket!

At FCC, students enjoy the top-class coaching provided by our vetted and experienced coaches, facilitating a wide variety of programmes for students of all abilities. Here at FCC, our cricketers enjoy excellent facilities including six cricket squares and a combination of all-year weather nets and stunning outdoor grass. We have recently introduced Under-15's and Senior girl's programme too led by our highly experienced and friendly women mentors. The classes currently offered are:

  • Under-5 Cricket Fun Club.
  • After school team-building skills(All age groups welcome)
  • Under-8 Cricket Coaching
  • Under-11 Cricket Coaching
  • Under-13 Cricket Sessions
  • Under-16's Programme
  • Under-15's Girl's Programme
  • Senior Girl's Programme
  • Under 19's Programme
  • Adult Seasonal Matches

Event-hires

Our facilities are available for off-seasonal and weekend private hires. Please check for the availability of our facilities on our calendar, which updates every 2 days. For group bookings or parties, please call our office or fill up the event booking-form on our "Bookings" page. For boardroom hires please call our office directly to speak to one of our event-managers.
